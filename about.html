About VouchTrack

Main street deserves the same machine the franchises have

The big chains have reputation teams, enterprise software, and agencies making sure every location gleams on Google. The salon owner competing with them has a front desk that's supposed to remember to ask. VouchTrack exists to fix that imbalance.

Hi, I'm Akshay Sudarsan

Connect with me on LinkedIn →

I started VouchTrack after seeing the same story repeat everywhere: genuinely excellent local businesses — the best cut in the neighborhood, the honest mechanic, the dentist patients drive across town for — losing new customers to a competitor with a louder Google profile. Not a better business. A better-reviewed one.

The fix isn't complicated. Ask every happy customer for a review, answer every review that comes in, and keep doing it every single day. The problem is that no owner has time to run that machine on top of actually running the business — and the software that promises to help just hands you another dashboard to ignore.

So VouchTrack is built as a service, not a tool. We use AI where it's genuinely brilliant — drafting replies in your voice, reading hundreds of reviews for patterns, tracking what ChatGPT says about you — and people where judgment matters. Nothing reaches your customers or your Google profile without a human saying yes.

And because I've sat through enough enterprise software sales calls for one lifetime: our pricing is published on the website, there are no contracts, and your first 30 days are money-back guaranteed. If we're good, you'll stay. That's the whole retention strategy.

— Akshay Sudarsan, founder

What we believe

Published pricing

If a company won't tell you the price before a sales call, the price is "whatever they think you'll pay." Ours is on the pricing page.

No locked doors

Month to month, cancel with one email. Annual billing exists only as a discount you choose — never a contract we hold over you.

A person, not a queue

You get a direct line to someone who knows your business by name. Ticket numbers are for the enterprise platforms.

AI with adult supervision

AI drafts, humans approve. Your reputation is too important for autopilot — and too much work for manual.

Main street first

Everything is built for single-location owners: salons, auto shops, dental offices, restaurants — not franchises with marketing departments.

Proof over promises

A monthly report card shows exactly what you're getting — and a calculator to sanity-check the math before you ever pay us.

Come say hello

Book a free 15-minute call — you'll talk to a person who'll pull up your Google profile and give you an honest read, whether or not we end up working together.

Pricing questions

What counts as a "text" or "email"?

Each review request we send on your behalf counts as one message. A typical customer receives one text or one email after their visit (plus at most one gentle reminder). If you serve 200 customers a month, you'll use roughly 200–400 messages — well within every plan's allowance.

Will my price go up later?

No. The rate you join at is the rate you keep. Founding Members lock in $199/month for as long as they stay subscribed — even after the founding rate closes.

Is there really no contract?

Really. Month to month, cancel with one email, effective at the end of your billing cycle. We keep customers by being good, not by locking doors.

What happens on the free demo call?

Fifteen minutes: we pull up your Google profile next to your closest competitor's, show you the gap in rating and review count, and walk you through exactly what VouchTrack would do about it. If it's not a fit, we'll tell you that too.

Lock in the founding rate

The first 15 businesses join at $199/month — for life. After that, the door closes and standard pricing applies.

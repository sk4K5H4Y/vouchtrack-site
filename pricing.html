Pricing questions

How does the founding rate work?

The first 15 businesses get our Standard plan at the Starter price: $199/month (or $159/month billed annually), locked in for as long as you stay subscribed. Once the 15 spots fill, Standard returns to $249/month for new customers — founding members keep their rate.

How does the annual discount work?

Choose annual billing and every plan drops 20%: Starter $159/mo, Standard $199/mo, Pro $239/mo — billed once per year. Founding members get Standard at $159/mo annual. It's entirely optional; monthly is the default and always will be. And the 30-day money-back guarantee applies to annual plans in full, so choosing annual never means risking a year's money.

How does the 30-day guarantee work?

Simple: if you don't want to keep VouchTrack — any reason at all — email us within 30 days of your first payment and we refund it in full. That applies to monthly and annual plans alike.

What counts as a "text" or "email"?

Each review request we send on your behalf counts as one message. A typical customer receives one text or one email after their visit (plus at most one gentle reminder). If you serve 200 customers a month, you'll use roughly 200–400 messages — within every plan's included allowance.

How do the message sliders work?

Every plan includes a monthly allowance of texts and emails. If your customer volume is higher, slide to add more: extra texts are $4 per 100 and extra emails are $4 per 1,000. Add-ons are always billed monthly — even on annual plans — so you can raise or lower them any time your volume changes.

What if I have more than one location?

$99/month per additional location, on any plan. Each location gets the same features as your base plan, its own review inbox, and the same done-for-you setup — billed on the same cycle as your plan.

Will my price go up later?

No. The rate you join at is the rate you keep. Founding members lock in Standard at $199/month (or $159/month annual) for as long as they stay subscribed — even after the founding spots fill.

Is there really no contract?

Really. Month to month, cancel with one email, effective at the end of your billing cycle. The annual option is a discount for prepaying — not a contract we hold you to, and it's covered by the same 30-day guarantee. We keep customers by being good, not by locking doors.

What happens on the free demo call?

Fifteen minutes: we pull up your Google profile next to your closest competitor's, show you the gap in rating and review count, and walk you through exactly what VouchTrack would do about it. If it's not a fit, we'll tell you that too.

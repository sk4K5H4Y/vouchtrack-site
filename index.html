How VouchTrack works

You keep running your business. We handle the reviews. Setup takes one phone call — you never touch a dashboard unless you want to.

Questions owners ask us

We already ask customers for reviews. Why do we need this?

Asking in person works — when it happens. But on a busy Saturday, nobody remembers. VouchTrack makes sure every single customer gets asked, automatically, at the moment they're happiest. Most businesses see more reviews in their first 60 days with us than in the previous six months of asking manually.

Is this a contract?

No. VouchTrack is month to month. Cancel anytime with a single email — no cancellation windows, no penalty fees, no hard feelings.

Do I have to learn new software?

No. We set everything up for you and it runs in the background. You'll get a simple monthly report card by email. If you ever want to log in and look around, you can — but you never have to.

What about negative reviews?

Two things. First, every negative review gets a prompt, professional response — which studies show matters more to future customers than the review itself. Second, our feedback flow gives unhappy customers a private way to reach you first, so problems get solved before they become public.

Is this only for Google reviews?

Google is the main event for local businesses, but we monitor and manage your reviews across Facebook, Yelp, and 85+ review sites — including the booking apps and industry directories specific to your business — all from one place.

How fast will I see results?

Review requests start going out in your first week. Most businesses see a meaningful jump in review volume within 30 days and a visible rating improvement within 60–90 days, depending on how many customers come through your door.

What if it doesn't work for my business?

Then you shouldn't pay for it. Every plan — monthly or annual — comes with a 30-day money-back guarantee: email us within your first 30 days and we refund everything, no questions, no forms.

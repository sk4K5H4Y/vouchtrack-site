The neighborhood ranking map

When someone searches "hair salon near me," Google's answer changes street by street. You might be #1 from two blocks away and invisible from the highway exit where new customers actually are. Our ranking map shows your position across a grid of points around your business — green where you're winning, red where you're not — and how it shifts month over month as your reviews grow.

It's the difference between "I think we're doing okay" and "we're #2 near the mall and it's improving."

Competitor watch

Pick the shop down the street. Every month you'll see their rating, review count, and pace next to yours — so you know exactly when you pass them, and they never sneak ahead without you noticing.

Friendly rivalry, weaponized.

AI reads every review, so you don't have to

Hidden inside a few hundred reviews are patterns no busy owner has time to find: which staff members get named and praised, which service keeps generating compliments, the parking complaint that shows up every August, the word "wait" creeping into your four-star reviews. Our AI reads everything — across all your review sites — and surfaces the patterns in plain English.

  • What customers rave about (and you should promote)
  • Which team members get named most — great for morale and retention
  • What's quietly costing you stars, before it costs you customers

The monthly report card

One email a month, readable in two minutes: rating trend, new reviews, ranking map movement, competitor comparison, and the AI's top insights. No dashboard required — though it's there if you want it.

It's also how you'll know, in plain numbers, that VouchTrack is paying for itself.

Owners ask us

What exactly is in the monthly report?

Your rating and review count trend, every new review and the reply we posted, your neighborhood ranking map with month-over-month movement, your competitor comparison, and the AI insights summary. Two minutes to read, forwardable to a business partner.

How does the ranking map work?

We check your Google ranking for your key search terms from a grid of points around your location — as if searching from each spot in the neighborhood — and plot the results on a map. Rerun monthly, so you can watch coverage spread as your reviews grow.

Do I need to log into anything?

No. The report card comes to your email, and alerts come by text or email as things happen. A full dashboard exists behind it all, and some owners enjoy poking around — but plenty of our happiest customers have never logged in once.

Owners ask us

Will widgets slow my website down?

No — it's a single lightweight embed built to load fast. If you'd rather not touch your site at all, we'll install it for you as part of setup; most installs take minutes.

Can I choose which reviews appear?

Yes. Widgets default to your best recent reviews, and you can pin favorites or hide any review from display. Curation is part of the service — tell us once how you like it and we'll keep it that way.

I don't have anyone who manages my website. Is that a problem?

Not at all — it's who we built this for. Send us your website login or introduce us to whoever set it up, and we handle the install. You'll just notice your reviews showing up.

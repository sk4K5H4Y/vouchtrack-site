Why "AI-powered" doesn't mean "robot-run"

AI is genuinely great at drafting replies, reading hundreds of reviews for patterns, and testing what ChatGPT says about you. It shouldn't be the last word on anything your customers see. At VouchTrack, it never is — a human approves every reply before it posts and sanity-checks every report before it lands in your inbox.

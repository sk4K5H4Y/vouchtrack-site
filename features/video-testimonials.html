From "would you mind?" to marketing asset

We ask for you

A video request goes out by text or email to customers who just had a great experience. They tap the link and record right from their phone — no app to download, no account to create.

Verified & displayed

Every video is collected first-party and clearly badged as verified. It appears alongside your written reviews in your website widgets — a face and a voice next to the stars.

Ready-to-post content

Each video doubles as social content for Facebook and Instagram — the kind of authentic post that stops the scroll, made entirely by your customers.

Built for businesses people can see

If your work is visual — a fresh cut, a set of nails, a spotless detail, a smile after the dentist — video is the most honest advertising there is. A stranger reading "great salon!" nods along. A stranger watching a delighted customer show off the result books an appointment.

And here's the quiet advantage: almost no local business does this, because asking feels awkward and collecting is clumsy. VouchTrack makes the ask for you and makes recording effortless — so the awkward part disappears and only the asset remains.

What your customer experiences

"We loved having you in today! Would you record a quick 20-second video about your visit? Tap here — it's easier than typing: [link]"

One tap opens their camera. They talk for twenty seconds, hit send, done. No app, no login, no typing. Sent from your business name, in your voice — VouchTrack is never mentioned.

A few great videos beat a hundred forgettable ads

Honest expectations: not every customer will record a video — and that's fine. This isn't a volume game like written reviews. A handful of genuine, verified customer videos on your website and social feed is a marketing asset most competitors simply don't have, and each one keeps working for years.

We suggest who to ask (your five-star reviewers and regulars are the natural yes), handle the asking, and curate what goes live — you approve everything before it appears anywhere.

Owners ask us

Will customers actually record videos?

Some will — and some is all you need. The ask goes to the customers most likely to say yes (your five-star reviewers and regulars), and recording takes twenty seconds on the phone already in their hand. Even a few genuine videos put you ahead of nearly every competitor in town.

Where do the videos show up?

On your website, in the same widgets that display your written reviews — clearly badged as verified customer testimonials — and as ready-to-post content for your Facebook and Instagram. You approve every video before it appears anywhere.

What if a customer records something I don't want public?

Nothing goes live without your approval. Every video lands in your dashboard first; you (or we, on your instruction) choose what gets published. Curation is part of the service.

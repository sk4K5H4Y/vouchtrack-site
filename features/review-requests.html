Timed for the happy moment

A review request that lands two weeks later gets ignored. One that lands while your customer is still admiring the haircut, or driving a car that finally sounds right, gets answered. VouchTrack times every request to your business — shortly after checkout, after the invoice, or after the appointment closes in your booking system.

If a customer doesn't respond, one gentle reminder follows a few days later. Never more. Nobody gets nagged in your name.

For the customer who doesn't know what to write

Plenty of happy customers tap your review link, stare at the empty box... and close it. Not because they're unhappy — because writing is work. On the Pro plan, an AI assistant inside the feedback form helps them put the experience into their own words: they pick what they loved, the assistant drafts it, they edit and post. Same genuine customer, same real opinion — just without the blank-page problem.

Pro also adds video review requests — for the customers whose face says it better than their typing ever could.

Why this matters

Every abandoned review box is a five-star that never happened. Removing the effort of writing is the single easiest way to turn more of your requests into posted reviews — and every review stays the customer's own words, approved by them before it goes anywhere.

No fake reviews, ever. The assistant helps real customers express a real opinion — it never invents one.

Owners ask us

Does it work with my booking software?

Almost certainly. If your system can send an email — a receipt, an invoice, an appointment confirmation — we can trigger review requests from it automatically. On your setup call we'll connect it for you; you don't touch anything.

Is texting my customers allowed?

Yes, done right. Requests go only to your own customers — people who gave your business their number — using templates with proper identification and easy opt-out. We set all of that up as part of onboarding so it's handled correctly from day one.

Can I control what the messages say?

Completely. We draft templates in your voice on the setup call, you approve them, and nothing changes without your say-so. Requests come from your business name — customers never see VouchTrack.

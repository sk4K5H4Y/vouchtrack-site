Owners ask us

Will replies sound like AI wrote them?

That's exactly what the human check is for. The AI drafts from your voice profile — how formal you are, what you call your customers, whether you use emoji — and a person approves every reply before it posts. If a draft reads canned, it doesn't go out.

Can I approve replies myself?

If you want to, yes — some owners like approving replies to negative reviews personally, and we'll route those to you first. Most owners let us handle everything and skim the monthly report. Your call, changeable anytime.

What about fake or unfair reviews?

Both get a calm, factual public reply — readers are good at spotting unreasonable reviewers when the business responds gracefully. Where a review clearly violates platform rules (fake, spam, wrong business), we flag and report it through the platform's removal process too. No one can promise removal — be wary of anyone who does — but reporting plus a professional reply covers you both ways.

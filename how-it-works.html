How it works

One phone call. Then it just runs.

VouchTrack is a done-for-you service, not software you have to learn. Here's exactly what happens from the moment you book a call — and exactly how little of it lands on your plate.

From first call to running quietly

The 15-minute call

We pull up your Google profile next to your closest competitor's and show you the gap — rating, review count, and pace. If VouchTrack isn't a fit, we'll say so and you keep the analysis.

We set everything up

One onboarding call: we connect your review sites, wire into your booking or invoicing tool, write your message templates in your voice, and set up your website widgets. You approve the wording — that's your whole job.

Requests start going out

Within your first week, every customer starts getting a friendly review request by text or email after their visit, plus a QR code for your counter. Automatically, every time, even on your busiest days.

Every review gets answered

New reviews — good or bad — get a reply within hours: drafted by AI in your voice, approved by a human before it posts. Unhappy customers get a private path to you before they ever reach the stars.

You watch the needle move

One monthly report card: rating trend, new reviews, your neighborhood ranking map, and how you stack up against the shop down the street. Two minutes to read, no dashboard required.

It compounds

Reviews build on reviews — more stars mean more clicks, more customers, more requests going out. The gap between you and the competition starts working in your direction for a change.

What you'll actually notice

  • Week 1: requests going out, first new reviews arriving
  • Day 30: a clearly higher review pace than before — this is where the money-back window closes, so you'll know by then
  • Day 60–90: visible rating movement and better placement when you search yourself
  • Month 4+: new customers saying "you had the best reviews" at checkout

The 30-day money-back guarantee

Try VouchTrack for a full month. If you're not seeing more reviews — or you just don't want to keep it — one email refunds every dollar. Monthly or annual, same guarantee.

Timelines vary with how many customers walk through your door — a busy salon moves faster than a seasonal HVAC shop — which is exactly what we'll size up honestly on your first call.

Owners ask us

How much of my time does this take?

About an hour total to get started: the 15-minute demo call, then a 30–45 minute onboarding call where we set everything up together. After that, your involvement is reading a two-minute report once a month — and occasionally approving a reply if you've asked to sign off on negative reviews personally.

Do I need to install anything or learn a dashboard?

No. Everything runs on our side. There's a dashboard if you ever want to look around, but plenty of our happiest customers have never logged in once.

What do you need from me to get started?

Access to your Google Business Profile (we'll walk you through granting it — takes two minutes), a sense of how customers flow through your business, and how you like to sound. We handle the rest.

What if I already have some bad reviews?

Almost everyone does — it's the starting point, not a problem. Old negative reviews get professional replies (readers care more about your response than the review), and the steady flow of new five-stars pushes your average up and the old ones down the page.

Fifteen minutes, no pressure: your profile next to your closest competitor's, the exact gap, and what we'd do about it. If it's not a fit, you'll know that too.

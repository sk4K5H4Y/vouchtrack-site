Reviews are your seasonal insurance

When the first heat wave hits, every HVAC shop's phone rings — but the overflow, the new-to-town homeowners, and the "my old guy retired" customers all go to whoever looks best on Google in that moment.

Building your review profile in the slow season is how you win the busy one. A shop completing 100 jobs a month that captures even 15% adds 180 reviews a year — enough to dominate a service area.

Owners and office managers ask us

Our jobs close in the field. How does the request go out?

However works for your workflow: automatically when the invoice sends, when the ticket closes in your system, or from a simple end-of-day list your office manager approves in one click. We set this up with you on the onboarding call.

Price complaints get calm, factual replies that explain your value without arguing — "licensed, insured, guaranteed work" reads very differently to a future customer than silence or defensiveness. And our private feedback flow catches many of these before they go public.

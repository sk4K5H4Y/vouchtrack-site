Landscaping owners ask us

Will weekly-service customers get asked every week?

No — frequency rules space requests out so recurring clients are asked occasionally and only until they’ve reviewed. One-time installs and cleanups get one well-timed ask with a gentle reminder.

My business is seasonal. Does this still work?

Seasonality is exactly why automation beats memory: requests peak with your busy months automatically, and the review base you build each summer is what fills next spring’s schedule before the trucks even roll.

What about a customer arguing over a bill or scope?

The private feedback flow gives them a direct channel to you first, where most disputes get settled with a conversation. Public negatives get calm, professional replies that never argue scope line-by-line in front of future customers.

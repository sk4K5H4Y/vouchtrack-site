Practice owners ask us

Is this HIPAA-safe?

Asking is fine — patients choose what to share. The replies are where practices get burned, and ours are HIPAA-aware by default: never confirming someone was a patient, never mentioning prescriptions or conditions, always human-approved before posting.

Most of my patients come through insurance lists. Do reviews matter?

Insurance gets you listed; reviews get you picked. Patients with a list of five in-network names choose by star rating almost every time — a strong profile is how you win patients you’d otherwise never meet.

What about complaints over glasses pricing or insurance?

Those frustrations get a private feedback path to you first, where a quick call usually fixes them. Public negatives that appear get calm, professional replies that never argue billing in public — which reads as competence to everyone else.

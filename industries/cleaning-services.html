Cleaning company owners ask us

Will my weekly clients get asked over and over?

No — frequency rules mean recurring clients get asked occasionally and only until they’ve reviewed. First cleans and deep cleans get one perfectly-timed ask with a single gentle reminder.

Do requests come from my company’s name?

Yes — every text and email is in your company’s name and voice. Clients never see VouchTrack anywhere; it reads like the friendly follow-up you’d send if you had the time.

What if something was missed on a clean?

The private feedback flow catches it first — most misses become a quick re-clean instead of a public complaint. Reviews that do post get gracious, accountable replies that make prospects trust you more, not less.

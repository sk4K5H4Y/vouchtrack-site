Moving company owners ask us

Damage claims happen. How do reviews handle them?

Head-on and calmly. The private feedback flow routes complaints to you first, while a fair resolution is still possible. Public damage reviews get accountable, human-approved replies that show future customers how you behave when something goes wrong — which is exactly what they’re checking for.

When is the best moment to ask after a move?

Not the night of — the day after, once the relief beats the exhaustion. Requests go out automatically on the schedule you set, with one gentle reminder for the customers who meant to but forgot.

Do requests come from my company’s name?

Yes — every text and email is in your company’s name and voice. Customers never see VouchTrack anywhere.

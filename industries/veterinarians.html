Reviews roll in

Grateful pet parents are the most willing reviewers in any industry — they just need to be asked. We ask every one, every time, so your count climbs from week one.

New families choose you

When someone new to town searches “vet near me,” your rating, volume, and visible kindness in every reply make you the clinic they call first.

Clinic owners ask us

Some of our cases end sadly. How is that handled?

With care. Requests can be held back for sensitive visit types you define, and euthanasia or emergency cases are never sent a cheery text. When a grieving client does write a review, the reply is compassionate, human-approved, and never defensive.

Do requests come from our clinic’s name?

Yes. Every text and email comes from your clinic’s name, written in your voice — even mentioning the pet’s name when your system provides it. Clients never see VouchTrack anywhere.

What if an owner was unhappy about a bill?

Billing frustration is the most common source of vet reviews that sting. Our private feedback flow catches it early and routes it to you directly — and when a public review does appear, the reply acknowledges the concern professionally without litigating the invoice in public.

Salon owners ask us

My clients are regulars — do reviews still matter?

Regulars keep the lights on, but growth comes from new faces — and every new client starts with a Google search. Reviews are also your best defense when a stylist leaves and tries to take clients: a strong salon brand outlasts any one chair.

Can requests come from my salon's name?

Yes. Every text and email comes from your salon's name, written in your voice. Clients will never see VouchTrack mentioned anywhere.

What if a client had a bad experience?

Our feedback flow gives unhappy clients a private way to tell you first — so you can fix it with a call or a redo before it becomes a public one-star. And when negative reviews do appear, they get a prompt, professional reply that makes you look good to everyone reading.

Med spa owners ask us

Is it appropriate to ask clients for reviews in this industry?

Yes — asking for an honest review is welcome in aesthetics, and most delighted clients are glad to share. What matters is discretion: requests come from your clinic’s name, never mention the treatment, and clients choose what they write. Nothing is ever incentivized or gated.

How do replies handle client privacy?

Every reply is written to be privacy-aware by default: it never confirms someone was a client, never references a procedure, and thanks them in general, gracious terms. Drafted by AI in your voice, approved by a human before it posts.

What about the occasional unfair review?

Our private feedback flow gives an unhappy client a direct line to you first — often that’s a follow-up appointment instead of a public one-star. When a negative review does appear, it gets a calm, professional response that reads well to every prospective client scrolling past it.

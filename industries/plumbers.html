Plumbing company owners ask us

My techs won’t remember to ask. Does this depend on them?

No — that’s the point. Requests go out automatically when the job is marked done, in your company’s name. Your techs fix pipes; the system does the asking, every single time.

What about “the price was too high” reviews?

Price gripes get two treatments: a private feedback path that catches many before they go public, and calm, professional replies for the ones that don’t — the kind that make the next reader think “reasonable company” instead of picking sides.

Do requests work for emergency and after-hours jobs?

Yes — timing adapts to the job. A 9 PM emergency fix triggers a next-morning request, when the customer is rested and grateful, not mid-crisis. You set the rules once during setup.

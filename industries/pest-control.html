Pest control owners ask us

Will recurring customers get pestered with requests?

No — frequency rules prevent that. Recurring accounts get asked occasionally and only until they’ve reviewed; one-time jobs get one well-timed ask with a single gentle reminder. Nobody gets nagged in your name.

How do you handle “the bugs came back” reviews?

The private feedback flow catches most of these first — usually the fix is a re-treat visit, not a public dispute. Reviews that do post get calm replies committing to make it right, which reads as accountability to every prospect scrolling.

Do requests come from my company’s name?

Yes — every text and email is in your company’s name and voice. Customers never see VouchTrack anywhere.

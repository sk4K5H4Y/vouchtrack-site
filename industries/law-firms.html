Attorneys ask us

Is soliciting client reviews permissible for lawyers?

Asking for honest reviews is generally permitted — what the profession’s rules universally prohibit is incentivizing them, writing them yourself, or misleading content. VouchTrack never incentivizes, never gates, and asks only for honest feedback. Your state bar’s specific guidance always governs, and we’re happy to work within it.

How do replies protect confidentiality?

Every reply is written to be privilege-safe: it never confirms someone was a client, never references a matter, and thanks the reviewer in gracious, general terms. Drafted by AI, approved by a human before posting — always.

What about reviews from opposing parties who were never clients?

They get calm, professional responses that note — without engaging on substance — that the reviewer wasn’t a client, which is often grounds to flag the review to Google as well. Either way, prospects see composure, not a courtroom.

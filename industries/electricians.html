Electrical contractors ask us

Most of my work comes from GCs and referrals. Why bother?

Because homeowners verify referrals on Google before calling, and general contractors do the same before adding a new sub. A strong profile converts the referrals you already earn — and brings in the direct residential work with the best margins.

Can requests wait until the inspection passes?

Yes — you choose the trigger. Many electricians send the request after final walkthrough or passed inspection, when the customer’s confidence is highest. Set it once; it runs forever.

What happens with an unfair review?

The private feedback flow catches most frustrations before they go public. Reviews that do land get a calm, factual, professional reply — approved by a human — that reads as competence to every homeowner scrolling past.

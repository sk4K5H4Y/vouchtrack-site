Roofing company owners ask us

Insurance-claim jobs get messy. How do reviews handle that?

Claim frustration is the top source of unfair roofer reviews. Our private feedback flow routes it to you first while there’s still time to fix the relationship — and public negatives get calm, factual replies that never re-litigate the claim in front of future customers.

My jobs take weeks. When does the request go out?

You pick the trigger — most roofers use final walkthrough or final payment. One request, perfectly timed, sent automatically, with a gentle reminder a few days later if needed.

Can requests come from my company’s name?

Yes. Every text and email is in your company’s name and voice — homeowners never see VouchTrack anywhere. It reads like a proud local builder following up, because that’s exactly what it is.

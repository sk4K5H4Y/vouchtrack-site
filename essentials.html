What these plans deliberately don't include

We'd rather tell you plainly than have you find out later. Essentials plans don't include text-message review requests (texting is the single biggest driver of new reviews — it's the engine of our main plans), the 67+ review sites inbox (Essentials covers Google, which is where the fight is won, but not Facebook, Yelp, Booksy and the rest), website review widgets, the neighborhood ranking map and competitor watch, AI insights, or AI search visibility tracking.

If reviews start working for you — and they will — you can move to any main plan with one email. Nothing to migrate, nothing to re-set-up. Your history comes with you.

Fair questions

What's the catch?

No catch — just less product. These plans handle the reviews you already receive; they don't go out and generate new ones the way our main plans do (that's the part with the texting engine, the 67+ sites, and the insights). Same team, same human approval on the responses that matter, same guarantee. Just a smaller machine.

Why isn't this on your main pricing page?

Because for most businesses it's the wrong plan — the revenue math overwhelmingly favors actively collecting reviews, not just responding to them. We offer Essentials case-by-case when budget is genuinely the blocker, because we'd rather start small with you than not start at all.

Can I upgrade later — and does my price change?

Anytime, with one email. You move to the current published rate of whichever main plan you choose — and if founding spots are still open when you upgrade, you can claim one. Downgrading back is allowed too. No penalties in either direction.
